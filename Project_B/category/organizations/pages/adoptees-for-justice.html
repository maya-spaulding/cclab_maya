Adoptees for Justice

Adoptees for Justice is an intercountry adoptee organization about working for justice for adoptees in immigration, racial, and social justice areas.

This group focues on activism, unlike other groups, which includes, fighting for the Adoptee Citizenship Act and other policies and legal channels to improve the legal status of international adoptees in the United States as the US does not have many protections for international adoptees.
