Organizations


Adoptees for Justice

Adoptees for Justice is an activist organization fighting for equal rights for international adoptees within the United States.


Also Known As

Also Known As is an international adopteee organization created for adult adoptees to find community.


China's Children International

China's Children International is an adoptee created and led organization for Chinese-adoptees of all ages to find community among adoptees without the shadowing of adoptive parents.


Families with Children from China

Families with Children from China is an adoptive parent organzation created for adoptive parents to find community when they adopt Chinese babies.
