International Child Search Alliance (ICSA)

International Child Search Alliance is an organization of adoptees and adoptive parents who provide resources for Chinese-adoptees searching for their biological parents. This resource may appear daunting at first because they have a lot of information and guides. However, it is really intuitive and you will be thankful for the amount of information later.

Resources include translation services, group searching ads, WeChat guides, DNA testing, and more.

Thank you for looking at my project and I hope it helps you as it once helped me in the past - Maya
