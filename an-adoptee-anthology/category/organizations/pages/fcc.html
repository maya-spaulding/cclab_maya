Families with Children From China (FCCNY)

Families with Children from China is a Chinese-adoptee parents' organization created for and by adoptive parents. Since its founding, over the years, the group has been shifting its focus to an adoptee-focus direction.

The founding chapter is FCC New York. However, there are FCC chapters across the country.

In their Facebook Page and Group, adoptive parents, adoptees, and others are allowed to post and make comments. This organization primarily was for adoptive parents with young children. However, with the drastic decline of adoptions from China, the organziation has been on the decline.
