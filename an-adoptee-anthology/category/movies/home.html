Movies


Blue Bayou

A Focus Features film about a Korean-adoptee at risk of deportation


Found

A Netflix docuentary released in 2021 about Chinese-adoptees finding out they're related to each other and decide to search for their birth parents


Lion

A film based on a true story about an Indian-adoptee searching for his lost family


One Child Nation

An independent documentary film about the one-child policy and its ramification on China and the world


Somewhere Between

An independent documentary about Chinese-adoptees growing up in the United States


The Half of It

A fictional film about a high school girl helping a classmate write to his crush, starring a Chinese-adoptee


To All the Boys I've Loved Before Series

The movie adapation of the book series by the same title, starring a Vietnamese-adoptee


Twinsters

A documentary film about Korean-adoptees discovering that they are twins
