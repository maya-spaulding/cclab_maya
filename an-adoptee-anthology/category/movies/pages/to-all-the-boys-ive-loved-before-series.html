To All the Boys I've Loved Before: Series

To All the Boys I've Loved Before is a book series written by Jenny Han. The story follows a biracial high school girl as she faces the effects of having her secret love letters sent to each crush including her sister's ex-boyfriend.

The reason why this is included in the moive section, however, is because the actress playing the main character, Lara Jean, is Lana Condor, a Vietnamese-adoptee.
